02 — About Me

THE STORY SO FAR

I'm a Partner Success and Customer Success professional with 6+ years of progressive experience in SaaS and Fintech, currently managing strategic partner relationships at Motive — one of the leading fleet management platforms in North America.

"Promoted four times in under four years — not by chance, but by choosing every challenge that pushed me to grow."

My career began at i2c Inc., a global fintech company, followed by a brief role as PR Officer at Zameen.com before transitioning into SaaS at TCP Software. At Motive, my journey has been one of consistent upward progression: CSM → Senior CSM → Solutions Consultant → Partner Success Manager.

Across my Customer Success career I've worked across all market segments — SMB and Mid-Market at TCP, then growing through Mid-Market at Motive before managing the more complex Enterprise (ENT) and Strategic (STRAT) books. As Solutions Consultant, I extended expertise into deployment and implementation rollout — designing installation plans, coordinating partner networks, and ensuring seamless go-live execution for high-ARR deals.

I've managed and expanded a book of business worth $12 million USD, pioneered the Account Handoff process between pre-sales and post-sales, and built QBR playbooks now used across the partner network.
